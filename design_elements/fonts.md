# Font Pairings

Reference mood: dark editorial, designer fashion, Undercover/Jun Takahashi, archivepdf.net.
Source: classic type combinations from print design. All mapped to Google Fonts equivalents.

> To select a pairing, reference its ID in `page_designs.md` under `FONT`.

---

## FONT-1 — "High Fashion Serif"
> Bodoni + Futura. The typeface pairing of Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, archivepdf.net covers. Maximum editorial tension between thick/thin serif and geometric sans.

| Role    | Font              | Google Fonts source                                          |
|---------|-------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| Heading | **Bodoni Moda**   | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Bodoni+Moda)        |
| Body    | **Jost**          | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Jost)               |
| Mono    | **JetBrains Mono**| [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/JetBrains+Mono)     |

**Original pairing:** Bodoni Std · Futura  
**Size scale:** large aggressive display headings, compact body  
**Letter spacing:** tight on headings (−0.02em), slight tracking on body  
**Weight range:** 200–300 body / 700–900 headings

## FONT-2 — "American Editorial"
> Garamond + Franklin Gothic. The editorial authority pairing — warm old-style serif meets workhouse gothic sans. Newspaper mastheads, long-form magazine layouts.

| Role    | Font               | Google Fonts source                                          |
|---------|--------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| Heading | **EB Garamond**    | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/EB+Garamond)        |
| Body    | **Libre Franklin** | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Libre+Franklin)     |
| Mono    | **JetBrains Mono** | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/JetBrains+Mono)     |

**Original pairing:** Adobe Garamond Pro · ITC Franklin Gothic  
**Size scale:** large warm headings, restrained body  
**Letter spacing:** minimal on headings, light on body  
**Weight range:** 300 body / 500–700 headings

## FONT-3 — "Stark & Condensed"
> Caslon + Trade Gothic Condensed. American serif authority meets a tall condensed gothic — stark, architectural, editorial. Strong vertical rhythm.

| Role    | Font                   | Google Fonts source                                          |
|---------|------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| Heading | **Libre Caslon Text**  | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Libre+Caslon+Text)  |
| Body    | **Barlow Condensed**   | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Barlow+Condensed)   |
| Mono    | **IBM Plex Mono**      | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/IBM+Plex+Mono)      |

**Original pairing:** Adobe Caslon Pro · Trade Gothic LT Std Condensed  
**Size scale:** bold headings, tight condensed body at small size  
**Letter spacing:** normal on headings, wide tracking on body (uppercase labels)  
**Weight range:** 300–400 body / 700 headings

## FONT-4 — "Fragile Elegance"
> Warnock + Akzidenz-Grotesk. Delicate high-contrast serif meets a neutral Swiss grotesque. Used in high-end Japanese fashion editorial and slow-luxury brands — suits "warmth discovered through uncovering."

| Role    | Font                    | Google Fonts source                                          |
|---------|-------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| Heading | **Cormorant Garamond**  | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cormorant+Garamond) |
| Body    | **DM Sans**             | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/DM+Sans)            |
| Mono    | **Fira Code**           | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Fira+Code)          |

**Original pairing:** Warnock Pro · Akzidenz-Grotesk  
**Size scale:** understated — detail is in the letterform, not the size  
**Letter spacing:** wide on headings (0.01–0.02em), normal on body  
**Weight range:** 300 body / 300–600 headings (avoid 900 — breaks the delicacy)

## FONT-5 — "Structural Slab"
> Clarendon + Univers. Slab serif authority meets a clean neutral grotesque. Structural, grounded, more technical — balances the solutions engineer identity with editorial weight.

| Role    | Font            | Google Fonts source                                          |
|---------|-----------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| Heading | **Zilla Slab**  | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Zilla+Slab)         |
| Body    | **Work Sans**   | [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Work+Sans)          |
| Mono    | **IBM Plex Mono**| [Link](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/IBM+Plex+Mono)     |

**Original pairing:** Clarendon LT Std · Univers Light  
**Size scale:** bold structural headings, clean readable body  
**Letter spacing:** slight negative on headings, normal on body  
**Weight range:** 300 body / 600 headings
