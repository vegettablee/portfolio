# Color Palettes

Reference mood: dark, ambient, stoic minimalism — Undercover by Jun Takahashi, archivepdf.net editorials.

> To select a palette, reference its ID in `page_designs.md` under `PALETTE`.

---

## PALETTE-A — "Undercover Black"
> Pitch black with warm paper-white text and a muted blood red accent. The closest to Jun Takahashi's runway lookbooks — stark, severe, but with a raw warmth in the type.

| Token             | Role                | Hex       | Note                            |
|-------------------|---------------------|-----------|---------------------------------|
| `--bg`            | Primary background  | `#080808` | near-black, no blue undertone   |
| `--surface`       | Surface / card      | `#131313` | barely lifted from bg           |
| `--text`          | Primary text        | `#E8E3D8` | warm off-white, paper-like      |
| `--accent`        | Accent              | `#B81C1C` | muted blood red — Undercover signature |
| `--accent-2`      | Secondary accent    | `#2E2E2E` | dark gray for borders/dividers  |

**Palette rule:** monochrome black with one red cut-through. Nothing else.

---

## PALETTE-B — "Archive Editorial"
> Deep warm charcoal with a parchment-cream text and an amber-gold accent. Feels like a scanned fashion editorial printed on aged stock — archivepdf.net energy. Less confrontational than A, more literary.

| Token             | Role                | Hex       | Note                            |
|-------------------|---------------------|-----------|---------------------------------|
| `--bg`            | Primary background  | `#0D0C0A` | warm near-black, slight sepia undertone |
| `--surface`       | Surface / card      | `#1A1815` | dark warm brown-black           |
| `--text`          | Primary text        | `#F0EAD6` | parchment cream                 |
| `--accent`        | Accent              | `#A07840` | muted amber-gold, aged paper warmth |
| `--accent-2`      | Secondary accent    | `#2C2A26` | border/subtle element tone      |

**Palette rule:** warm neutrals only — no cool grays, no pure black.

---

## PALETTE-C — "Cold Void"
> Cold obsidian black with an icy off-white and a steel-blue accent. More technical and architectural — fits the solutions engineer side. Feels like Undercover's more experimental, structural collections.

| Token             | Role                | Hex       | Note                            |
|-------------------|---------------------|-----------|---------------------------------|
| `--bg`            | Primary background  | `#080A0F` | cold near-black, slight blue-black |
| `--surface`       | Surface / card      | `#10131A` | dark navy-black                 |
| `--text`          | Primary text        | `#D8DDE8` | cool off-white with slight blue |
| `--accent`        | Accent              | `#4A6A7C` | muted slate blue — understated, architectural |
| `--accent-2`      | Secondary accent    | `#1C2030` | dark blue-gray for surfaces     |

**Palette rule:** cold monochrome with one muted steel accent — no warmth anywhere.

---

## Notes
- All three are dark-mode only
- None use pure `#000000` — slight tint prevents harshness
- Visual preview → `colors_preview.html`
